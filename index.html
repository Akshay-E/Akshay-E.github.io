>Hello, World!_

I am an MSc Physics graduate from India, working for the past year (Nov 2021- Oct 2022) as a junior research fellow in a joint project between the SETI research centre at UC Berkeley and Amity University, Mumbai. Through this project we are conducting India's first Search for Extraterrestrial Intelligence (SETI) using the upgraded Giant Metrewave Radio Telescope (uGMRT). I am interested in developing tools for radio astronomy, SETI, studying pulsars, radio transients and keen on expanding this list(!).
I believe in communicating knowledge to others outside of the scientific community and spent a significant part of my physics bachelor's degree volunteering at the Regional Science Centre and Planetarium in Calicut. I organised and conducted telescope sessions for young students, educated the general public about the science of skywatching and spent many a night stargazing. I have led and have experience in working with teams in a professional environment and have presented my research works at national and international conferences.
